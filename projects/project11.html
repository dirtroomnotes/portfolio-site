Infrastructure Modernization & Data Migration

Managed enterprise infrastructure modernization and secure data migration projects for government and defense-affiliated clients, deploying NetApp solutions to ensure compliance, uptime, and operational excellence.

Company: NetApp / SBS Partnership

Duration: Mar 2018 – Mar 2019

Tools: Pulse, MS Project, Excel, SharePoint, MS365 (Teams, Outlook), GSDC Dashboards

Platform: Enterprise Infrastructure & Storage Solutions (SnapCenter, MetroCluster, StorageGRID)

Delivery Method: Waterfall

Summary: Led modernization of enterprise infrastructure, coordinated server/storage/network deployments, ensured regulatory compliance, and delivered high-availability solutions for sensitive government environments.

Work Done

  • Managed infrastructure modernization and data migration projects valued $500K–$10M, including DoD-affiliated deployments.
  • Coordinated rack/stack, configuration, and cut-over validation of servers, storage arrays, and networking equipment.
  • Partnered with network engineers to verify connectivity, routing, and failover readiness for minimal downtime.
  • Scheduled and executed server migrations with risk mitigation and post-migration validation.
  • Maintained project schedules, status reporting, and technical documentation for visibility and compliance.
  • Facilitated collaboration between on-site technicians, vendors, and remote teams to meet infrastructure milestones on time.
  • Ensured regulatory compliance and audit readiness; passed DoD and federal audits with zero findings.
  • Delivered comprehensive program documentation including knowledge transfer guides, SLAs, and dashboards.
