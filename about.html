About Me

Professional Journey

Well, Hello there! I’m **Traci**, a Project Manager and Business Analyst who loves turning big, messy projects into clear, successful outcomes. For over seven years, I’ve worked across SaaS, ERP, cloud, IoT, and infrastructure, helping teams cut through the noise, find focus, and feel confident in what they deliver. My approach is simple: **create clarity, build trust, and empower people**. I thrive in environments where collaboration is key, and I enjoy mentoring teams, guiding them through complex projects, and celebrating successes along the way.

Education & Growth

I love finding ways to grow personally and professionally. I utilize the perspective from my **Psychology** background to think outside the box, fostering a **people-first mindset** in technical environments. Combined with my rigorous **Data Analytics** coursework and certifications, these experiences reflect both my technical expertise and my commitment to strategic problem-solving.

I also enjoy exploring new tools and techniques, from automation strategies to process optimizations, to ensure every project I touch delivers meaningful results. I often share these insights and lessons on my blog, connecting my professional experiences with creative problem-solving strategies.

Personal Passions Fuel Professional Success

Outside of work, I’m driven by curiosity and creativity. My pup Sonnie 🐾 makes sure I get fresh air and a healthy dose of play, music 🎶 is my constant source of inspiration, and time with family keeps me grounded. I love discovering new hobbies, whether it’s experimenting with tech projects at home, diving into a good book, or exploring local art and culture. Sharing what I learn through my blog allows me to connect with others and contribute to the broader community of curious minds.

I’m a big believer that personal passions fuel professional success. Whether it’s tackling a challenging project, brainstorming creative solutions, or coaching a teammate through a tricky workflow, I bring the same energy, empathy, and curiosity that drives my life outside of work. I enjoy collaborating with teams who value clarity, accountability, and innovation, and I’m always looking for opportunities to learn from others while sharing my own insights.
